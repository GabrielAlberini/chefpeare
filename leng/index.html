About us?

Verónica Peteán

Verónica Peteán is an Actress and English Teacher. In addition, she is an instructor in Ashtanga Yoga, and She is a student at the Santa Fe Provincial Theater School. Her acting training is based on from 2016 to the present in the Laboratory of Theatrical Anthropology (Santa Fe), coordinated by Ana Woolf (Odin Teatret-Bs As / Denmark), who Produced, among others, Cantata of Land and Sea, Love and Damn Training. Since 2017 she has been a member of the group Beso de Mariposa, miniature puppets, collective of women who are dedicated to the manufacture, and Lambe Lambe box handling. She has done animation work for events of public and private character. She highlighting her work as a presenter and entertainer.

Pedro Peterson

Pedro Peterson is an actor, musician, lawyer and cultural manager. His acting training has as base the study on stage presence based on Theater Anthropology (Eugenio Barba-Julia Varley-Ana Woolf), the clown, improvisation and live music. He has carried out acting work in theater and for television, conducting performances musical scenic, directing and co-directing works for concerted groups and casts. In his role as cultural manager he worked on the design and execution of the National Festival of Interdisciplinary Art: ENTREVERARTE (2014-2017) and the Cycle: Teatro en la Cortada (2021). He was trained in English language -higher level-, has two International exams issued by the University of Cambridge.

Team Bio

Paraná - Entre Rios - Argentina

Team Chefpeare was born at the beginning of 2018 with the purpose of investigating the historical cultural heritage bequeathed by William Shakespeare and whose study allows the construction of bilingual dramaturgy and the production of theatrical works.

In 2019 he premiered “Chefpeare: Theatrical Recipes of William Shakespeare”.

This show has been represented in different contexts and with varied audiences, confirming its good reception from different viewers. Adults, adolescents, and children, as well as teachers and language students, and the public without knowledge of English have immensely valued the proposal, highlighting it for its creativity, ingenuity, versatility, and educational content.

In turn, the group has managed to establish links with higher level academic institutions and universities to carry out various artistic-pedagogical activities.

Show

The protagonists enter the scene with a light and happy choreography to the rhythm of a French chanson. They are: Chef Veronique de la Creme & Chef Jean Pierre Panzón “disciples of the wonderful English writer”. Veronique is a cook determined to “follow the recipes to the letter”, nothing is left to chance. Everything is measured, controlled and planned. Jean Pierre, on the other hand, es a charismatic chef, a bit clumsy but a good cook, he believes that the kitchen serves to improvise and create new flavours.

They are the hosts of a cooking magazine style program, in which they present dishes from a Shakespearean menu that they will cook live mixing the literary universe with the gastronomic one. What these chefs still do not know is that this combination will also mix their own worlds, creating something unique and unrepeatable: their own love story.
